E
Containment System / Septic Tank
E1. What type of onsite sanitation system do you have? *
This is the underground tank or pit where your toilet waste goes. A septic tank is usually a concrete box; a pit is a hole in the ground (lined or unlined).
E1a. Please specify other sanitation type
E2. WALL construction: Sealed or Porous? *
Sealed = concrete/masonry with plaster; Porous = brick without plaster, stone
E3. BOTTOM construction: Sealed or Not sealed? *
Sealed = concrete slab at bottom; Not sealed = open to soil
E4. Does the tank have internal partition wall(s)? *
A partition is a wall inside the tank that divides it into 2 or more chambers. Select "Don't Know" if you're unsure.
E5. How many partition walls inside the tank?
E6. Is there any OUTLET from the tank? *
An outlet is a pipe coming out of the tank that lets liquid flow out. Check if there is any pipe leading away from your tank.
E7. Where does the outlet discharge to? *
Where does the liquid from the outlet pipe go? A soak pit absorbs it into the ground; an open drain is a visible channel.
